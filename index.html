G'day, I'm Nick from Sydney,

In 2016 I followed my interest in tech, user experience and product to the thriving tech scene of Berlin. I'm currently a Product Manager, overseeing the development and launch of a new IOS and web app while attending as many tech, product and coding meet-ups as I can.

I have a passion for health, fitness and competition; I'm a representative on the Australian Ultimate Frisbee team, winning Bronze at the 2016 World Ultimate Championships. When I'm not training for or playing ultimate, I love exploring nature, skiing and developing websites.

 
 

I love designing, creating and problem solving!

For 8 months I assisted binaere bauten's software team as a frontend development intern. I was part of a four-person team that developed a currently used app from scratch. It was such a fantastic experience contributing to a product through its complete life cycle, from concept to release.

Technology wise, my main focus has been HTML, CSS, Javascript & JQuery, which I've used to build this website. I have dabbled in Anjular2, XML & Typescript.
