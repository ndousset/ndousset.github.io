G'day, I'm Nick from Sydney, Australia

In 2016 I followed my interest in tech, user experience and product to the thriving tech scene of Berlin, Germany. I'm currently a Product Manager, overseeing the development and launch of the new IOS and web app überliste. I attend as many tech, UX, product and coding meet-ups as I can.

I have a passion for health, fitness and competition; I'm a representative on the Australian Ultimate Frisbee team , winning Bronze at the 2016 World Ultimate Championships. I love travelling and the experience of being immersed in different cultures and landscapes.

 
 

Studying front-end development and the art of coding has been an amazing eye opener into the world of design, user experience and product.

For 8 months I assisted binaere bauten's software team as a frontend development intern. I was part of a four-person team that developed a currently used app from scratch. It was such a fantastic experience contributing to a product through its complete life cycle, from concept to release.

Technology wise, my main focus has been HTML, CSS, Javascript & JQuery, which I've used to build this website. I have dabbled in Anjular2, XML & Typescript.

 
 

I have always thought critically about the challenges people face and the products which solve their problems.
